How we work

Six things we don't bend on.

01

One team, end to end

Discovery, build, and go-to-market run under one roof. Nothing gets lost in a handover between three vendors who have never met.

02

A name, not a queue

Every area has a directly responsible individual. You always know exactly who owns your project and how to reach them.

03

Start at any phase

Come with the plan settled and only need the build. Come with a finished product that isn't selling and only need go-to-market. The rest is there when you need it.

04

Built to last, not to demo

Everything we ship comes with a documented API, so it connects to whatever comes next — with or without us in the loop.

05

Limited projects per quarter

The DRI model doesn't scale by piling projects onto the same people. So we cap the quarter instead. It costs us revenue; it's the difference between a studio and a queue.

06

The groundwork is the work

Research, teardowns, and first drafts are not a formality before the real project. They are what stops the real project being a guess.
